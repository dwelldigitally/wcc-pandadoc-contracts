# PandaDoc Folders & Organization

## Document Folders

- Organize into nested folders (e.g., by semester, program, department)
- Drag-and-drop bulk actions for moving many documents
- API: `POST /public/v1/documents/{id}/move-to-folder/{folder_id}`

## Template Folders

Keep templates organized separately:
- "Enrollment Agreements"
- "Financial Aid Forms"
- "Housing Contracts"
- "Program Addenda"

## Workspaces

Each workspace is a fully isolated environment with its own:
- Dashboard
- Documents
- Templates
- Catalog
- Contacts

**Suggested workspaces:**
- Admissions
- Financial Aid
- Housing
- Academic Programs

## Tags

- Tag documents and templates by theme
- Enables searching across workspaces
- Use for cohort tracking (e.g., "Fall-2026", "Nursing-Program")

## Best Practices

1. Put outdated/draft templates in an "Archive" folder
2. Label templates with dates/versions: "Enrollment Agreement - Fall 2026 v2"
3. Limit who can create/edit templates to prevent content sprawl
4. Use consistent naming conventions across all documents
