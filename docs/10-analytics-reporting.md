# PandaDoc Analytics & Reporting

## Document Dashboard

Real-time overview showing:
- Document counts and values by status (draft, sent, viewed, completed)
- Filterable by week/month/quarter/year
- Filterable by team member

## Key Metrics

### Document Funnel
Visualize conversion rates:
```
Sent → Viewed → Completed
```

### Time-to-Close
- Average time from document sent to completed
- Identify bottlenecks in enrollment process

### Close Rate
- Percentage of sent documents that reach completion

### Per-Member Metrics
- Documents sent, viewed, completed per team member
- Values associated with each

## Recipient Engagement
- Number of views per recipient
- Time spent on each page
- Engagement level scoring

## Export & Integration

### CSV Export
- Export document activity reports for specific date ranges

### BI Integration
- Connect to Tableau or Power BI for advanced analysis

### Automated Reports
- Schedule recurring reports to stakeholders

## Enrollment Analytics Strategy

1. **Monitor enrollment funnel:** Sent vs. Viewed vs. Signed
2. **Identify stragglers:** Students who viewed but didn't sign → follow up
3. **Track time-to-sign:** Optimize enrollment timeline
4. **Weekly reports:** Dashboard screenshots to leadership during enrollment periods
5. **Export to BI tools:** Cross-reference with other enrollment metrics
